## How It Works

### 1. Submit an Idea

Anyone can submit an idea by [opening an issue](https://github.com/shivankar-madaan/notbuiltyet/issues/new?template=idea.yml). The template asks you to fill in:

- **Category** â€” Healthcare, Agriculture, Education, Infrastructure, Finance, Environment, Logistics, or Other
- **Idea Title** â€” A clear, concise name
- **Problem & Solution** â€” What's broken today and how AI fixes it
- **Estimated Improvement** â€” How much better this could be (e.g. "~60% improvement over current")
- **Competitive Moats** â€” What makes this defensible (data moat, network effects, regulatory, technical, domain expertise, first mover, integration depth)
- **Viability Estimate** â€” How realistic is this (90-100%, 75-89%, 50-74%, 25-49%)
- **Overall Defensibility** â€” Very High, High, Medium, or Low

You don't need to have all the answers â€” submit what you have and the community will help refine it.

### 2. Vote and Discuss

Browse the [open issues](https://github.com/shivankar-madaan/notbuiltyet/issues) and give a ğŸ‘ reaction to ideas you think are worth building. Leave comments with feedback, market research, technical insights, or domain expertise. The best ideas rise to the top through community discussion.

### 3. Vetting

Maintainers review discussed ideas and add the `vetted` label to those that are well-researched and viable. Once an idea is vetted, it automatically appears on the website with its viability score, moat analysis, and vote count.

### 4. Build It

See something you want to build? Go for it. No permission needed. If you're actively working on an idea, let us know and we'll add the `being-built` label. When you ship it, we'll mark it as `launched`.
